We love whitewater rafting

We believe that there is nothing like a river trip for relaxing. That is why we subscribe to the dry oar philosophy of boating. Keeping your oars dry for us means taking time to look around and notice the beauty that surrounds the rivers we love.

Come Relax with Us

We invite you to come relax with us on one of the amazing rivers we visit Whether you are an experienced paddle sports enthusiast or just looking for some fun on the water, you've come to the right place. Come enjoy a different desert experience! It's a family friendly group activity, No Alcohol or Smoking Allowed.

Come Run with us.

No matter your level of expertise, this tour will give you a great day out! If you are looking for a way to relax in a beautiful environment, this professionally guided tour is for you. You'll get to enjoy natural hot springs, easy kayaking, stunning waterfalls and simply magnificent views
