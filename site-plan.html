Overview

Purpose

We’re dedicated to sourcing the highest quality spices, herbs and botanical products through our growers, employees and their families. Our employees are from local communities and work for our company for a long time. We provide child care for our employees and the posibility to have lunch with their kids. We are family and comunity centry. Our Mission is to feed the world and contribute to a healthy life. We constantly invest on improving the quality of our producst while distributing at an affordable price. We believe in community work and a give back for all the support we receive from the comunity.

Audience

Our products are for you that enjoy cooking and having a healthy diet. We promote slow food. Dedicating the appropriate time to enjoy a good meal, conversation with friends and family. We invite you to use our product and experience the power of having food with good taste, smell and nutrition contribution to your body. We want you to be healthy and enjoy life with your love ones. Our products are for you, family and friends. A healthy community start with a healthy individual. Our contribution to your well being is our company goal and the quality of our products is the outcome of it.

The Site Map of a site is just like it sounds…it is a map of the pages in a site and how they are related and linked together. From the map above we can see that we will eventually have the Home page and 2 sub or child pages.

The lines that connect them all together indicate that each page should be accessible from any other page, it is essentially showing us the global navigation for the site.

Wireframes

Wireframes are like blueprints for making webpages. They should show the major sections of content that will be on the page and the relative locations of each element. In the wireframe below you can see there will be 6 sections to our page:

  1. At the top we have a section with the logo (the box with the mountain means an image) and the navigation bar.
  2. Then there is a banner image that stretches all the way across the screen.
  3. Next we have some text and an image
  4. ...followed by another row made up of an image and some text.
  5. Then one more section of text with no image.
  6. Lastly, a footer containing a copyright/name line and 3 social media icons.
